Device Info Privacy Policy

Package name: io.bright.deviceinfo

Device Info does not collect or store any personal user data directly. However, the app integrates third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Analytics (Google Analytics). These services may automatically collect some non-personal information to provide ads, analyze app usage, and help improve app performance.

Third-Party Services Used

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. AdMob may use your Advertising ID and cookies to personalize ads and measure ad performance. Learn more at Google Ad Policies.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used for crash reporting and stability monitoring to help identify and fix app issues. Learn more at Firebase Privacy.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used to understand app usage and improve the experience. It collects anonymous usage data. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

All data collected by these third-party SDKs is processed securely and is not personally identifiable. Device Info itself does not access, store, or share any personal user information.

Required Permissions

  • INTERNET: Required to access online content and display ads.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Used to check network connectivity status.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: Used to display Wi-Fi information.
  • BLUETOOTH: Used to access Bluetooth features for device testing.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Used to show network and SIM-related information.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: Used to display the list of installed apps on your device.
  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used to test the microphone and speaker functionality.
  • VIBRATE: Used to test vibration functionality.

Permissions are only used for their stated purposes and are never misused to collect private data. Some permissions may be automatically granted by Android.
